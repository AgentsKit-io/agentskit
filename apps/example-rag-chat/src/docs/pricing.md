# Nimbus pricing

Nimbus has three plans. You can change plans at any time; changes take effect
at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Free

- 5 GB storage
- 50 GB egress per month
- 1 project
- Community support

The Free plan is permanent — it does not expire after a trial period.

## Pro — $19 / month

- 500 GB storage included, then $0.02 per GB
- 1 TB egress per month included, then $0.05 per GB
- Unlimited projects
- Email support with a 24-hour response target
- Signed URLs and custom domains

## Scale — $99 / month

- 5 TB storage included, then $0.015 per GB
- 10 TB egress per month included, then $0.04 per GB
- Priority support with a 4-hour response target
- SSO / SAML and audit logs
- 99.9% uptime SLA

## Billing notes

Egress is metered hourly and billed monthly. Storage is measured as the average
of hourly snapshots over the billing period. Unused included quota does not roll
over to the next month.
